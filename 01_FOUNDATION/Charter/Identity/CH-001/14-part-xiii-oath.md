# Part XIII – The Oath

## Chapter 13 – The AURA Oath

This section will contain the formal oath or code of commitment for founders, engineers, researchers, and contributors.

Example commitments:
- I will build with integrity.
- I will protect privacy and security.
- I will remain accountable for my work.
- I will serve human well-being first.
- I will uphold the principles of AURA.
