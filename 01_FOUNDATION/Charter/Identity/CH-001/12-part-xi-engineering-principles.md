# Part XI – Engineering Principles

## Chapter 11 – The Twenty-Five Engineering Principles of AURA

This section is reserved for the formal definition of AURA’s twenty-five engineering principles.

Suggested categories:
- Architecture and design
- Reliability and performance
- Security and privacy
- Documentation and collaboration
- Testing and quality assurance
- Maintainability and evolution

AURA will define each principle in subsequent revisions of this charter.
