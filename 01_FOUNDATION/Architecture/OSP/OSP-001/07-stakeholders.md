# OSP-001 — Stakeholders

---

# 1. Purpose

This section identifies every stakeholder who influences, contributes to, or benefits from the AURA ecosystem.

Stakeholder identification ensures that architectural decisions align with technical, organizational, and user expectations.

---

# 2. Internal Stakeholders

## Project Founder

Responsibilities

- Strategic Vision
- Product Direction
- Final Architecture Approval

---

## Core Architecture Team

Responsibilities

- Enterprise Architecture
- Knowledge Architecture
- Design Governance
- Architecture Reviews

---

## Engineering Team

Responsibilities

- Backend
- Frontend
- APIs
- Infrastructure
- Security

---

## AI Research Team

Responsibilities

- Model Selection
- Evaluation
- Optimization
- Knowledge Systems

---

## Documentation Team

Responsibilities

- Technical Documentation
- Standards
- Specifications
- Release Documentation

---

# 3. External Stakeholders

- Open Source Contributors
- Academic Researchers
- Students
- Organizations
- Developers
- AI Communities

---

# 4. Stakeholder Responsibilities

Every stakeholder shall:

- Follow Engineering Standards
- Maintain Documentation
- Participate in Reviews
- Respect ADR Decisions
- Support Continuous Improvement
