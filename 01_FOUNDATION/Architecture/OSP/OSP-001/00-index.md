# Purpose

The Organization & System Plan (OSP-001) defines the strategic architectural foundation of the AURA ecosystem.

It serves as the primary architectural blueprint that governs the design, organization, engineering direction, and long-term evolution of AURA.

Every subsequent architectural document—including Enterprise Architecture (AEA), Knowledge Architecture (AKA), Engineering Standards (AES), Design Specifications (ADS), Architecture Decision Records (ADR), Architecture Assessment Reports (AAR), and the Architecture Research Program (ARP)—derives its guidance from OSP-001.
