# Purpose

This document defines the organizational structure, architectural vision, engineering strategy, governance principles, and long-term system planning for the AURA platform.

It serves as the primary architectural reference for every subsystem developed under the AURA ecosystem.

All future architecture documents—including Enterprise Architecture (AEA), Knowledge Architecture (AKA), Engineering Standards (AES), Design Specifications (ADS), Architecture Decision Records (ADR), Architecture Assessment Reports (AAR), and Research Program (ARP)—shall derive their principles and organizational alignment from this document.

# Authority

OSP-001 is the highest-level architectural planning document within the AURA Foundation.

In the event of conflicting implementation decisions, the architectural guidance defined in this document shall take precedence unless superseded by an approved Architecture Decision Record (ADR).

OSP-001 is a living architectural document.
