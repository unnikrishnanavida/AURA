# AAR-001 — Risk Assessment

---

# Purpose

Risk Assessment identifies architectural risks that may affect the success of the AURA platform.

---

# Risk Categories

- Technical Risks
- Security Risks
- Operational Risks
- Infrastructure Risks
- Data Risks

---

# Risk Evaluation

Each risk is evaluated based on:

- Probability
- Impact
- Severity
- Mitigation Strategy

---

# Risk Management Principles

- Early Detection
- Continuous Monitoring
- Preventive Action
- Regular Review

---

# End of Risk Assessment
