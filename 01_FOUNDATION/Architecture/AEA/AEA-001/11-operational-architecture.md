# AEA-001 — Operational Architecture

---

# 1. Purpose

Operational Architecture defines how the AURA platform is monitored, maintained, supported, and continuously improved after deployment.

---

# 2. Operational Objectives

The operational model shall:

- Ensure system availability
- Monitor health
- Detect failures
- Support incident response
- Enable continuous improvement
