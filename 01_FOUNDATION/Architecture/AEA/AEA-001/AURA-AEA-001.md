> ## Document Purpose
>
> This document is the official master specification for the Enterprise Architecture (AEA-001) of the AURA platform.
>
> It consolidates every enterprise architecture chapter into a single authoritative specification that defines the structural organization, application landscape, data architecture, technology stack, security model, deployment strategy, governance, and operational framework of AURA.
>
> The modular chapter documents remain the editable source of truth, while this master specification serves as the official release artifact for publication, review, versioning, and distribution.
