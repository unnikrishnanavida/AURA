# AEA-001 — Enterprise Overview

# Purpose

This section introduces the overall enterprise architecture of AURA and explains how organizational capabilities are mapped to software systems and infrastructure.

---

# Enterprise Vision

AURA is designed as an enterprise-scale AI platform capable of supporting intelligent services across research, software engineering, education, automation, and decision support.

---

# Enterprise Domains

The enterprise consists of six architectural domains:

- Business
- Applications
- Data
- Technology
- Security
- Operations
