# Authority

AEA-001 serves as the authoritative enterprise architecture specification for AURA.

All application, infrastructure, data, deployment, and technology decisions shall align with this document unless superseded by an approved Architecture Decision Record (ADR).

---

# Revision Policy

Every architectural modification shall include:

- Revision Number
- Change Description
- Author
- Approval
- Date
