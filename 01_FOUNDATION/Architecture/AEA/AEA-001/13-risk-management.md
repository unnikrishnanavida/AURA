# AEA-001 — Risk Management

---

# 1. Purpose

This document defines the enterprise-wide strategy for identifying, evaluating, mitigating, and continuously monitoring risks that may impact the AURA platform.

---

# 2. Risk Categories

## Technical Risks

- Software Defects
- Infrastructure Failure
- AI Model Drift
- Performance Bottlenecks

---

## Security Risks

- Unauthorized Access
- Data Leakage
- API Abuse
- Credential Exposure

---

## Operational Risks

- Downtime
- Backup Failure
- Monitoring Gaps
- Human Error

---

## Business Risks

- Resource Constraints
- Knowledge Loss
- Regulatory Changes
- Community Sustainability

---

# 3. Risk Management Process

Identify

↓

Assess

↓

Prioritize

↓

Mitigate

↓

Monitor

↓

Review

---

# 4. Mitigation Strategies

- Redundancy
- Monitoring
- Automated Testing
- Documentation
- Disaster Recovery
- Security Audits

---

# 5. Continuous Review

Enterprise risks shall be reviewed:

- Quarterly
- After major releases
- After security incidents
- During architecture reviews

---

# End of Risk Management
