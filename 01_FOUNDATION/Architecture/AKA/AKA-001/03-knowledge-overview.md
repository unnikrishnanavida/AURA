# AKA-001 — Knowledge Overview

# Purpose

This chapter introduces the conceptual foundation of knowledge within AURA.

Knowledge is treated as a strategic enterprise asset that powers intelligent decision-making, autonomous reasoning, and continuous learning.

```
Data

↓

↓

↓

Improved Knowledge
```

# Knowledge Principles

- Knowledge is contextual.
- Knowledge is connected.
- Knowledge evolves continuously.
- Knowledge must be explainable.
- Knowledge should be reusable.
- Knowledge must be verifiable.
