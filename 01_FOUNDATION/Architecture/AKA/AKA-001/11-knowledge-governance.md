# AKA-001 — Knowledge Governance

---

# 1. Purpose

Knowledge Governance establishes policies and controls for managing knowledge quality, ownership, security, compliance, and lifecycle management.

---

# 2. Governance Objectives

- Maintain Knowledge Quality
- Ensure Traceability
- Protect Sensitive Information
- Standardize Metadata
- Support Compliance

---

# 3. Governance Structure

```
Knowledge Owner

↓

Knowledge Steward

↓

Knowledge Engineer

↓

AI Agents

↓

End Users
```

---

# 4. Governance Policies

- Version Control
- Approval Workflow
- Metadata Standards
- Retention Policy
- Archival Policy

---

# 5. Quality Assurance

Every knowledge object shall be evaluated for:

- Accuracy
- Completeness
- Consistency
- Timeliness
- Reliability
