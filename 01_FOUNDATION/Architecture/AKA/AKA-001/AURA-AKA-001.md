## Document Purpose

This master specification consolidates all chapters of the AURA Knowledge Architecture into a single authoritative document. It defines the cognitive framework that governs knowledge acquisition, representation, storage, retrieval, reasoning, memory, learning, governance, and security across the AURA platform.

The individual chapter files remain the editable source of truth, while this document serves as the official publication and release artifact.
