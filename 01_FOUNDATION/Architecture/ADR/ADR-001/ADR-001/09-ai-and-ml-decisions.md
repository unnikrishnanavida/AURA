# ADR-001 — AI and ML Decisions

---

# Purpose

This chapter documents architectural decisions related to Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) capabilities within the AURA platform.

---

# Decision Objectives

- Enable intelligent automation
- Support scalable AI services
- Ensure explainable AI
- Maintain modular AI components
- Enable future model upgrades

---

# Decision 001

## AI Model Integration

**Decision**

Support pluggable AI models through standardized interfaces.

**Status**

Approved

---

# Decision 002

## Inference Strategy

**Decision**

Use API-based inference while supporting local model deployment when required.

**Status**

Approved

---

# Decision 003

## Prompt Management

**Decision**

Maintain centralized prompt templates with version control.

**Status**

Approved

---

# Decision 004

## Model Versioning

**Decision**

Every production AI model shall have a unique version identifier.

**Status**

Approved

---

# Decision 005

## AI Monitoring

**Decision**

Monitor model performance, latency, accuracy, and resource utilization.

**Status**

Approved

---

# AI Principles

- Model Independence
- Explainability
- Reproducibility
- Continuous Improvement
- Responsible AI

---

# End of AI and ML Decisions
