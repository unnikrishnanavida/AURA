# ADR-001 — Executive Summary

---

## Overview

Architecture Decision Records (ADRs) provide a structured approach for documenting significant architectural choices throughout the AURA platform's lifecycle.

They ensure every major technical decision is traceable, justified, and understandable for future developers and stakeholders.

---

## Objectives

- Document architectural decisions
- Improve traceability
- Maintain design consistency
- Support future enhancements
- Reduce technical debt

---

## Decision Areas

- Technology Selection
- Architecture Patterns
- Security
- Data Management
- AI Integration
- Deployment
