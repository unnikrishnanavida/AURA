# Purpose

The Architecture Decision Records (ADR) document serves as the official repository of significant architectural decisions made during the design, implementation, and evolution of the AURA platform.

Each decision captures the problem being addressed, available alternatives, the selected solution, rationale, consequences, and implementation status. This ensures architectural transparency, traceability, and long-term maintainability.

---

# Scope

This specification governs architectural decisions related to:

- Technology Selection
- Architecture Patterns
- Security
- Data Management
- Artificial Intelligence
- Deployment
- Integration
- Governance
