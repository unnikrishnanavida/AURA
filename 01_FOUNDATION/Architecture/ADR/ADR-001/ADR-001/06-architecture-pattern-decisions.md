# ADR-001 — Architecture Pattern Decisions

---

# Purpose

This chapter records decisions regarding architectural patterns adopted in AURA.

---

# Decision 001

Architecture Style

Decision:

Service-Oriented Architecture (SOA) shall be adopted.

Status:

Approved

---

# Decision 002

API Design

Decision:

API-First development shall be followed.

Status:

Approved

---

# Decision 003

Communication

Decision:

REST APIs shall be the primary communication mechanism.

Status:

Approved

---

# Decision 004

Scalability

Decision:

Components shall remain loosely coupled and independently deployable.

Status:

Approved

---

# End of Architecture Pattern Decisions
