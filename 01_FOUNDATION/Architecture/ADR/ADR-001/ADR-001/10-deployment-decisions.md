# ADR-001 — Deployment Decisions

---

# Purpose

This chapter records architectural decisions governing deployment strategies for the AURA platform.

---

# Decision Objectives

- Improve availability
- Enable scalability
- Simplify deployments
- Increase reliability
- Reduce downtime

---

# Decision 001

## Deployment Strategy

**Decision**

Adopt cloud-native deployment using containerized services.

**Status**

Approved

---

# Decision 002

## Infrastructure

**Decision**

Separate application services, AI services, databases, and monitoring infrastructure.

**Status**

Approved

---

# Decision 003

## Scalability

**Decision**

Horizontal scaling shall be preferred over vertical scaling.

**Status**

Approved

---

# Decision 004

## Availability

**Decision**

Critical services shall support High Availability (HA).

**Status**

Approved

---

# Decision 005

## Disaster Recovery

**Decision**

Automated backup and disaster recovery mechanisms shall be implemented.

**Status**

Approved

---

# Deployment Principles

- Cloud Native
- Fault Tolerance
- High Availability
- Automation
- Infrastructure as Code

---

# End of Deployment Decisions
