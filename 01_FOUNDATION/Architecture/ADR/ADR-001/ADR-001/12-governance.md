# ADR-001 — Governance

---

# Purpose

Architecture Governance ensures that architectural decisions remain consistent throughout the lifecycle of the AURA platform.

---

# Governance Objectives

- Maintain architectural consistency
- Reduce technical debt
- Ensure compliance
- Improve maintainability
- Standardize reviews

---

# Governance Roles

- Project Founder
- Architecture Board
- Technical Leads
- Development Team
- Quality Assurance Team

---

# Governance Activities

- Architecture Reviews
- ADR Approval
- Compliance Verification
- Design Audits
- Periodic Assessments

---

# Governance Policies

- Every significant architectural change requires an ADR.
- Approved ADRs become project standards.
- Deprecated ADRs shall remain archived for historical reference.
- ADRs shall be reviewed during major releases.

---

# End of Governance
