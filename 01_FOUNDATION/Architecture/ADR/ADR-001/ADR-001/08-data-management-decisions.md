# ADR-001 — Data Management Decisions

---

# Purpose

This chapter documents architectural decisions related to data management.

---

# Decision 001

Primary Database

Decision:

PostgreSQL shall serve as the transactional database.

Status:

Approved

---

# Decision 002

Data Format

Decision:

JSON shall be the standard API exchange format.

Status:

Approved

---

# Decision 003

Backups

Decision:

Daily automated backups shall be maintained.

Status:

Approved

---

# Decision 004

Data Retention

Decision:

Historical records shall follow defined retention policies.

Status:

Approved

---

# Design Principles

- Data Integrity
- Consistency
- Availability
- Security

---

# End of Data Management Decisions
