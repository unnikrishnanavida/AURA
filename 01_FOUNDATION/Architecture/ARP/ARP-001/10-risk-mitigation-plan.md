# ARP-001 — Risk Mitigation Plan

---

# Purpose

This chapter defines strategies to identify, evaluate, and mitigate architectural risks throughout the implementation roadmap.

---

# Risk Categories

- Technical Risks
- Infrastructure Risks
- Security Risks
- Operational Risks
- Project Risks

# Risk Management Process

```
Risk Identification

↓

Risk Analysis

↓

Risk Prioritization

↓

Mitigation Planning

↓

Monitoring

↓

Review
```

---

# Mitigation Strategies

- Preventive Controls
- Continuous Monitoring
- Automated Testing
- Backup and Recovery
- Disaster Recovery Planning

---

# Success Criteria

- Reduced Risk Exposure
- Faster Incident Recovery
- Improved System Stability
