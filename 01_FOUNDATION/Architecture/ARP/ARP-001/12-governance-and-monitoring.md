# ARP-001 — Governance and Monitoring

---

# Purpose

This chapter defines governance policies and monitoring mechanisms for successful roadmap execution.

---

# Governance Objectives

- Maintain Strategic Alignment
- Track Progress
- Manage Risks
- Ensure Compliance

---

# Governance Roles

- Project Founder
- Architecture Board
- Technical Leads
- Engineering Teams
- Quality Assurance

---

# Monitoring Activities

- Architecture Reviews
- KPI Tracking
- Risk Monitoring
- Compliance Audits
- Performance Reviews

---

# Success Indicators

- Roadmap Milestones Achieved
- High Compliance Rate
- Continuous Improvement
